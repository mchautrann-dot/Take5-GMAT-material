In a music contest, a panel of 100 audience members ranked each of 4 performers with integer ranks—from 1 to 4. For each performer, the table indicates the number of audience members who assigned each rank to the given performer. For example, exactly 49 audience members assigned rank 4 to Performer A. Two methods—the Mode Method and the Median Method—are used to assign final ranks to the performers based on these audience rankings. Mode Method: Assign to each performer a final rank equal to the audience rank the performer received most often. Median Method: Compute the median audience ranking for each performer. The ordering of the final rankings will be the same as the ordering of these medians. ­
